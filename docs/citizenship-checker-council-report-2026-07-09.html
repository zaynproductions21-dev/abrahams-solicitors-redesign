04 Blind spots the original spec missed

  • s.1(3) / s.1(4) automatic-British-at-birth branches for UK-born adults
  • GDPR lawful basis / consent on wizard-to-CTA data capture
  • Person / LegalService / FAQPage / HowTo schema markup
  • Visible dated solicitor byline on-page (not just in config)
  • Backlink outreach as a shipping workstream
  • Emotional register / opener acknowledging that nationality is personal
  • Route-appropriate CTAs — blanket "book a call" is unearned on simple routes

05 Recommended changes — v0.1 must-haves

  1. Static route explainers below the wizard. Each route as a crawlable H2 section with statute name, 150-250 words, FAQPage + HowTo schema.
  2. Author E-E-A-T + schema block. Visible "Reviewed by Imran Shah, SRA #509359" byline with dated stamp; Person schema with sameAs → SRA register; LegalService schema; References section citing BNA 1981 + NABA 2022 + Home Office guidance.
  3. Route titles human-first, statute-second. "You may be able to register as British because you were born here and lived here your whole life (legal basis: s.4B)".
  4. Add s.1(3) + s.1(4) coverage. Two extra questions on parents' current UK immigration status + UK residence since birth. Question count 6 → 8.
  5. Cap "not sure" branching. ≥2 "not sure"s → soft explainer, not immediate sales handoff.
  6. Lock URL + H1 + title-tag differentiation. URL /british-citizenship-registration-checker/; H1 "British Citizenship Registration Entitlement Checker"; title targets "registration" not "naturalisation"; cross-link both wizards.
  7. Route-appropriate CTAs. "Already British" → first-passport guide, no call. Simple registrations → documents guide + call. Discretionary routes (s.4L / s.3(1)) → call + Windrush-specific triage skipping standard CTA funnel.
  8. Routing decision matrix signed off by Imran BEFORE code. Explicit truth table Q1-Q8 → route.
  9. Hardcoded top-of-results disclaimer banner. "This is general information, not legal advice. No solicitor-client relationship is created until we accept your instructions in writing."
  10. Quarterly statute review cadence (not annual). Hard date in wizard config header, owned by Imran.

Deferred to v2 — captured, not blocking

  • Email nurture sequence per route
  • Paid tier CTA ladder (£29 letter / £149 review / £499 form-prep / £1500 full app)
  • 8 dedicated indexable pillar pages per route
  • Backlink outreach sprint (Windrush advocacy, Commonwealth associations, university student services)
